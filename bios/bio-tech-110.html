Brad Rubenstein

Dr. Rubenstein has trained, coached, and led organizations and project teams in technology and finance. He is a partner at Celerity Consulting Group, working with project teams internationally to improve their performance, delivering faster and more reliable results.

He is co-author (with Adam Josephs) of the book Risk Up Front: Managing Projects in a Complex World, (Lioncrest, 2018).

Dr. Rubenstein received his Ph.D. in Computer Science from the University of California (Berkeley, 1987). Among his many positions prior to joining Celerity, he developed database products as a member of technical staff at Sun Microsystems, and core infrastructure for derivatives risk management as a Vice President at Goldman Sachs.
